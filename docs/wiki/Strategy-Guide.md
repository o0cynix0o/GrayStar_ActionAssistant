# Strategy Guide

Spoiler warning: strategy pages discuss route structure, item priorities, and achievement targets.

The strategy guides do not replace the books. They are meant to help you understand the assistant support and avoid losing track of important resources.

## Available Guides

- [Book 1 Strategy Guide](Book-1-Strategy-Guide)
- [Book 2 Strategy Guide](Book-2-Strategy-Guide)

## Guide Style

Each guide focuses on:

- route shape
- item priorities
- risky mechanics
- useful assistant buttons
- achievement targets
- known manual or watch-list moments

The guides avoid copying book prose.
