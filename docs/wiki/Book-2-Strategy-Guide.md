# Book 2 Strategy Guide: The Forbidden City

Spoiler warning: this page discusses Book 2 structure, mechanics, items, and achievements.

## Goal

The goal of Book 2 is to survive the route to section 310 and complete the transition toward Book 3.

The assistant supports Book 2 with guided section support, flow buttons, combat presets, random-number helpers, gear capture and return, Backpack loss, achievements, and repeat-book flow.

## Route Families

The Book 2 audit identified these major route families:

- swamp and Chaksu route
- Karnali and Freedom Guild route
- dead lands, Samu, Hugi, and Deathgaunt route
- Forbidden City route
- final Shadow Gate route

Different route families unlock different achievements and item opportunities.

## Gear Loss And Recovery

Book 2 has both temporary gear loss and permanent Backpack loss.

Important modeled cases:

- section 149 stores unavailable Staff and Backpack gear
- section 200 restores that stored gear
- section 297 permanently discards Backpack contents and marks the Backpack unavailable

Temporary storage and permanent discard are different. The app treats them differently.

## Karmo Potion

The Karmo Potion remains partly manual.

Reason: the section gives the player timing discretion about when to apply the random Endurance penalty. The app records the potion and supports the roll, but the final timing choice should be handled by the player.

## Combat Advice

Use the Combat tab for fights instead of trying to remember every modifier from the text.

## Final Advice

Watch your Willpower closely. Book 2 has several moments where Magick, survival, and route choices all compete for the same resource.

Keep important route items visible in the Inventory tab, and use the app's guided controls for section-specific effects where support exists.
