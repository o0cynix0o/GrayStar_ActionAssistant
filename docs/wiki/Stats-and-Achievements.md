# Stats and Achievements

The app tracks book progress and achievements as part of the save.

## Achievement Types

Achievements can be tied to:

- book completion
- long route exploration
- combat milestones
- survival moments
- story routes
- discovery and item milestones
- gear loss and gear recovery moments

## Unlock Alerts

When an achievement unlocks, the app shows a browser notification-style alert so the player notices it during play.

Unlocked achievements also remain visible in the Achievements tab.

## Backfill

Achievements sync automatically from save history when the app loads or serves state.

That means if the save already contains durable evidence that you reached a section, completed a book, recovered gear, or triggered a story event, the app can unlock the achievement without a separate manual rebuild button.

## Replay Targets

Some achievements are naturally replay targets.

For example, if a route was never taken or a choice was never made, that achievement should stay locked until a later run.

## Repeat Book

Repeating a completed book resets Endurance and Willpower to base values for that book run, but keeps the same character and preserves already unlocked achievements.
