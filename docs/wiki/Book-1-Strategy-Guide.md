# Book 1 Strategy Guide: Grey Star the Wizard

Spoiler warning: this page discusses Book 1 structure, mechanics, items, and achievements.

## Goal

The goal of Book 1 is to survive the journey to the Book 2 transition at section 350.

The assistant supports this with section effects, combat presets, random-number helpers, gear loss and recovery, achievements, and the complete-book screen.

## General Priorities

Keep an eye on:

- Willpower, especially before Magick-heavy choices
- Endurance before fights and forced travel losses
- food supply
- Staff availability
- Backpack and Herb Pouch contents

Gray Star can spend Willpower quickly. Do not assume the app will stop you from making a risky book-legal choice.

## Inventory Advice

Useful item categories:

- **Weapons**: keep at least one weapon available.
- **Backpack Items**: food, rope, and other practical gear matter often.
- **Herb Pouch Items**: herbs and potions are easy to forget unless they are recorded.
- **Special Items**: story items and route keys should stay visible.

If the book says to note Backpack Items before losing the Backpack, the assistant stores the lost gear and can return it later when the recovery section is reached.

## Combat Advice

Book 1 has several fights with special rules. The assistant handles the important tested cases:

- Staff use at 0 Willpower
- no-weapon `-8 CS`
- Staff-unavailable `-6 CS` where the rules call for it
- Jewelled Dagger bonus only when selected
- shielded and unshielded combat variants
- mental combat with fixed Combat Skill
- one-round or threshold-based combat checks

Use the Combat tab instead of trying to track all round effects by hand.

## Achievement Notes

Book 1 achievements include journey, combat, survival, discovery, and story milestones.

Some achievements may remain locked after one successful run because they require routes or choices you did not take. Known replay-style targets include:

- Ezeran Acid Maker
- Redeemer's Tokens
- Shield Raised
- Leap of Faith

That is normal. Repeat Book is there for exactly this kind of cleanup run.

## Assistant Watch Points

- Use the Combat tab for special fights.
- Use the Inventory tab to confirm whether a potion or herb is in the Herb Pouch or Backpack.
- When a death happens, choose **Repeat** for another try at the same fight, or **Rewind** to make a different route choice.
