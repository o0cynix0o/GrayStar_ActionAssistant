## Major Route Families

- Swamp and Chaksu route: early swamp choices can lead through the Chaksu rescue and Chaksu Pipes.
- Karnali route: Sado/Freedom Guild choices lead to the Kazim Stone confrontation and the liberation of Karnali.
- Dead lands route: Samu/Hugi/Deathgaunt choices govern survival, Azawood use, and the approach to Gyanima.
- Forbidden City route: Gatekeeper, palace, Hugi, Black Rod, and magic-locked door routes converge on the Shadow Gate.
- Final Shadow Gate route: Black Rod choices at the Kleasa determine the successful transition to Book 3 or a terminal failure.
